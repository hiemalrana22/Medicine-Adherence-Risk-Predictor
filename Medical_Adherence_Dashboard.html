Key Finding: Refill Ratio is the strongest predictor of adherence in the ML models. Heart Disease patients show the highest refill ratio among adherent patients (0.805) but the lowest among non-adherent (0.728) — suggesting a clear behavioural split. 90-day supply patients show proportionally better adherence rates than 30-day supply patients.
